# 040 — governance readable conditions (PROPOSED)

This is the strongest motivation for Readable Logic Forms.

Audit reports and compliance reviews benefit most from conditions that read
as plain English business rules rather than operator symbols.

`amount is greater than or equal to Money.gbp("10000.00") and status is not Approved`
maps directly to: "If the amount is at least £10,000 and the payment is not yet approved."
